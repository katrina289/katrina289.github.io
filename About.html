    Kim Tuyet aka Katrina is a graphic designer, who has experience with motion graphic, user interface and books art.

    She is currently graduating from School of Art Institute of Chicago (SAIC), major in Visual Communication.

    Katrina previously graduated from Royal Melbourne Institute of Technology (RMIT) in 2017 and achieved the Bachelor of Communication.

    As an Asian artist who was inspired by observational realism, Katrina’s work focus on the emotions, storytelling and her own perspective.

    During her time at SAIC, Katrina has worked with book forms, motion graphic and user interface design. She is interested in using shape and color compositions to response to the issue/ insight gathering from the research stage.
