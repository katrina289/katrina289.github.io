Overview

This is a spec work that proposed a new identity systems and its application for ArtBash 2023 - an annual exhibition of Contemporary Practice Department of SAIC.

Concept

The contemporary practices for freshmen years at SAIC is the process of exploring different medium, tactics, concept, etc., and exploding various of your possibilities. Hence, the identity for ArtBash 2023 will become a Point Of Creation, which demonstrates the Big Bang moment of the Art Universe.

ArtBash becomes the starting point of the Art Universe, in which student will explode their creativity in 2D arts, 3D arts, virtual arts, films, performances, book arts, etc.

KEYWORDS: Big Bang, explosion, creation
