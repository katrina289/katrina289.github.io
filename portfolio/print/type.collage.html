Collection of 6 collages created from Chicago Loop Typography Texture | Spring 2020

    6’’x6’’ CARDBOARD This project emphasized the unique typeface texture of Chicago Loop. I collected photos of road signs, restaurant sign board, graphite drawing of Downtown Chicago and comprised them in a square frame to create overlapping and overwhelming feeling. Since most of the typeface is originally in lighter color, black hard-board was chosen to be the background for this collection.
