Joyfull & Happy Ornette Colemen | Fall 2020

    18’’x30’’ POSTER This poster is developed from the collage set. Joyful, passionate, and free is the main concept for the poster. In this project, I focus on blending and spiral shapes that reference Ornette Coleman’s music. Instead of blue shades, I keep the original color palette—red (shades) for the main palette and green–blue the spot color to depict the joyful and happy aspect of Ornette Coleman.
